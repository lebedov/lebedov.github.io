Psalms I

Size: 5" x 8"
Completed: 1998

I wrote the letters of this manuscript using Pelikan 4001 black ink and an Osmiroid calligraphy pen. The borders were colored with Daler Rowney Series A and B gouache and Windsor & Newton water-based gold ink.
